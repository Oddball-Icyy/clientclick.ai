FAQ

Questions roofing contractors actually ask us.

What makes ClientClick different from other agencies? +
We specialize exclusively in roofing — no generalist clients. We've built a complete deal generation system covering lead generation, AI qualification, automated booking, and reputation management in one integrated stack. And we back it with a results guarantee.
How quickly can I expect to see results? +
Most clients see their first booked appointments within 7–14 days of launch. Emergency repair is a high-intent vertical — when a homeowner has a storm-damaged roof, they need someone now. Our system intercepts that urgency at exactly the right moment.
What does "exclusive per market" actually mean? +
Once we accept you as a client, we will not onboard another roofing contractor targeting the same zip codes. We can't run conflicting strategies for competing companies — it would undermine your results. First claimed, first protected.
Do I need to be tech-savvy to use your system? +
No. The Done-For-You and Done-With-You tiers require zero technical involvement from you. We build everything, configure everything, and manage everything. You need to know how to answer your phone and show up to estimates.
What is your guarantee exactly? +
If we don't hit the agreed KPIs in your first 30 days, we work for free until we do — or we refund you. We define the KPIs together before we start so there's no ambiguity. We've never had to invoke this guarantee, but it exists so you have zero risk.
What ad platforms do you use? +
We primarily use Meta (Facebook + Instagram) and Google Search for lead generation. For the right accounts, we also deploy TikTok and run cold email outreach through Clay.com. The mix depends on your market, budget, and roofing niche.
How does the AI lead qualification work? +
When a lead comes in through any channel, our AI responds within 60 seconds via SMS or chat. It asks qualifying questions, gauges urgency, answers objections, and either books the appointment or flags the lead for your team to handle manually.
Can I start small and upgrade later? +
Absolutely. Many clients start with Tier 01 or Tier 02 to test the waters, then upgrade as they see results. Each tier is designed to layer on top of the previous one — your investment compounds as you scale up the stack.
